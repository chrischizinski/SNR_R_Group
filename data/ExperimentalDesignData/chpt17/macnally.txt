Format of the files: (Box 17-3, Box 18-4, Fig 17-4, Fig 18-5)
	* macnally.xls (Microsoft Excel)
	* macnally.csv (comma delimeted ascii text file)

File contents:
	* SITE - sites in Southern Australia from which seasonal abundances of 102 species of birds were measured
	* HABITAT - habitat type of each site
	* V1.. through V102.. - abundances of birds
	* COMPCOR1 - PCA correlation component 1
	* COMPCOR2 - PCA correlation component 2
	* COMPCOR3 - PCA correlation component 3
